üìà Build Credibility

  1. Send Personalized Connection Requests

  2. Welcome Message (Non-Commercial)

  3. Increase Visibility Through Content
    Share HPS insights, engage with their posts, establish thought leadership
  4. Commercial Interaction Only When Relevant
    Wait for signals of interest or timing triggers before making commercial outreach
